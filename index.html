How to play
This game is designed for two players. Both players should choose different sides of the screen (which is red or blue). When a player presses their designated key, (which is A or L by default,) their side extends and pushes the other's side away. The objective is to push the other player's side off-screen, so press your key as fast as you can!
Both players should only use one finger for pressing thier key during the entire round (unless they both agree to do otherwise).
